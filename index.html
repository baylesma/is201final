Maren Bayles

Why should she be your best friend?

Finishing her sophomore year of college at Brigham Young University in Provo, Utah, Maren Bayles calls Bentonville, Arkansas home, although China was home for a long time. Maren was born in Laguna Hills, California to Scott and Marcie Bayles in August of 2001. She lived there with her parents and two older siblings until right before her third birthday, when they decided to move to Shenzhen, China in 2004 for her fathers job. They lived there for six years until 2010 when Walmart employment moved them to Bentonville, Arkansas where her family still resides.

After high school graduation, Maren went to a year of school at Brigham Young University before going on a year and a half volunteer mission for The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. She served the Chinese people in the area of Newport Beach, California where she really solidified the learning of the Manderin language.

She soon returned to Brigham Young University and is currently planning on studying Accounting at the BYU Marriott School of Business with a minor in Asian Studies. She is currently on studying abroad in Taipei, Taiwan this upcoming summer of 2023.
